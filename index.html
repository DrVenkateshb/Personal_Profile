About

Dr Venkatesh B received his B.Tech (Computer Science and Engineering) from Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Ananthapur, Andhra Pradesh in 2009. He received his Master of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering from Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Ananthapur, Andhra Pradesh in 2013. Received Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in School of Computer Science and Engineering from Vellore Institute of Technology, Vellore. He is currently working as an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at BVRIT Hyderabad College of Engineering for womens. He has published more than 10 technical papers in various international journals, conferences, and book chapters. He is associated with many professional bodies like IAENG, CSTA, SDIWC, CSI. His research interest includes Machine Learning, Pattern Recognition, Data Mining, Data Analysis, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing.
